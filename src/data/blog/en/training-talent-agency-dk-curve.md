A short trip back to Taiwan means running private training sessions nonstop⋯

Today I was at a talent agency, walking their planners, producers, and finance people through using Claude Code for contract review, reconciling receipts and accounts, and closeout reports⋯

Watching people who only knew how to use chatbot AI yesterday command agentic AI collaboration with full confidence today, it just makes me happy!

But they're right now on the steep upslope of the DK confidence curve, so my next task is to deftly catch the moment they start heading down and help them make a soft landing, while topping up on the less appealing but important concepts of security, permissions, compliance, and version control⋯

That'll be the challenge for the next class.
